New Hire Orientation

What to expect during
your first three days

NHO spans 3 live virtual mornings. Sessions wrap by midday. Your afternoons are yours for role-based onboarding, async learning, and getting settled in your tools.

When to log on

  • 8:00 AM PST · 9:00 AM MST · 10:00 AM CST · 11:00 AM EST
  • EMEA: sessions begin at 4:00 PM GMT / 5:00 PM CET
  • Sessions run approximately 3 hours each morning. Afternoons are yours.
  • Your calendar invite has the Zoom link and full agenda. Review it before Day One.
  • Zoom link: auditboard.zoom.us/my/aborientation

Before you log on

  • Have your laptop powered up and connected to stable internet
  • Find a quiet-ish space. Camera on is expected for all live training sessions.
  • Dress code is casual. Comfortable and camera-ready is all we ask.
  • Keep your personal email open on Day One. Your first 3 NHO invites are sent there. The rest come to your Optro email once your account is active.
  • Headphones with a mic make a real difference

Who you'll meet

  • Ashlynn Woods, People Experience Team
  • Your direct manager
  • Your department team
  • Your onboarding buddy
  • IT & Systems team
  • Your fellow Optronauts (new hire cohort)

Need help on Day One?

  • Tech issues? Your IT contact is in your onboarding email
  • Can't join? Email onboarding@auditboard.com immediately
  • Laptop trouble? We'll troubleshoot live. No stress.
  • Coming into the office? Let your team know and we'll make sure someone greets you

Culture & Values

How we work.
And why it matters.

Our values aren't a poster on a wall. They shape how we make decisions, communicate across time zones, and show up for each other and our customers every day.

01

Customer obsession

It starts and ends here. Consistently ask yourself how what you're doing creates value for our customers. It's not just a priority. It's a mindset that shapes every decision we make.

02

Gritty resilience

Make it happen. Find a way. We move fast, stay positive, and do what it takes. When things get hard (and they will), we don't slow down. We find a path through.

03

Drive innovation

Learn, be bold, evolve. We create the future. Continuously improve what exists and invent what's next. Comfort with the status quo is not something you'll find here.

04

Win, together

One crew. No silos, no egos. We drive to be the best and we support each other's success. A win for a fellow Optronaut is a win for all of us. Full stop.

05

Growth mindset

10x, not 10%. We think in orders of magnitude, not increments. Seek feedback, learn, and improve. Ambition here is measured by how much bigger we can go.

+

Community at Optro

We enable belonging through Optronaut-led resource groups that drive connection and inclusion across the company.

WOO DIBs Culture Committee

Distributed by default, not by accident

Optro is a distributed-first company. Remote isn't a compromise, it's the design. We default to async, document everything, and make sure no one is disadvantaged for not being in a room. If you come into the office, expect it to be quiet. Most of your fellow Optronauts are online.
