Structure & Governance

Governance Body
DIBS
DIBS is the central governance body for ERGs, led by the People Experience team. It provides structure, oversight, and support so ERGs can operate with consistency.
  • Mentors and supports ERG leaders
  • Manages the centralized programming budget
  • Reviews and approves funding proposals
  • Hosts quarterly cross-ERG syncs
  • Connects ERGs to Internal Comms and People Team
Community Leaders
ERG Leaders
ERG leaders set the vision, build programming, and cultivate belonging within their community. Each ERG designates leads across active timezones.
  • Define the ERG mission and goals
  • Plan events and engagement opportunities
  • Submit programming proposals to DIBS
  • Coordinate across timezone leads
  • Participate in cross-ERG leadership syncs
Senior Leadership
Executive Sponsors
Executive sponsors provide visibility and strategic guidance for each ERG, amplifying ERG work within leadership conversations.
  • Align ERG programming with company priorities
  • Increase visibility of ERG initiatives
  • Participate in select events and discussions
  • Advocate for ERG communities within leadership

Starting a New ERG

1
Submit an Interest Form
Any Optro employee can request a new ERG using the form below. Describe the community, why it matters, and your initial interest in leading it.
2
Build a Leadership Team
New groups need a core leadership team and a mission statement. Each ERG must designate a lead for each active timezone from the start.
3
DIBS Review and Approval
DIBS reviews the proposal alongside the People Team and leadership sponsors. Groups are assessed based on employee interest and capacity.
4
Onboarding and Launch Prep
Approved ERG leaders join DIBS onboarding and receive launch resources, templates, and guidance on submitting their first programming proposals.
5
Soft Launch
The ERG soft launches with initial programming, communications coordinated with Internal Comms, and a community channel set up for all timezones.
